Three.js browser driving prototype

Urban Drive

Drive a modern sport coupe through a stylized city with smoother handling, lane traffic, world collisions, missions, and a retractable mini map.

Controls
  • W accelerate
  • S brake / reverse
  • A / D steer
  • Space handbrake
  • M toggle mini map
  • R reset car
Systems
  • Lane-based traffic
  • Mission checkpoints
  • Third-person follow camera
  • Obstacle and vehicle collisions

Best run from a local server such as VS Code Live Server.
